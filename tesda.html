MY TESDA JOURNEY

My journey as a TESDA student has been a wonderful experience for me through the years that have passed. I started as a Computer System Servicing NC II student and got my first training on May 11, 2021. It was a big adjustment since I had no idea about computers ever since. This might be hard for us since we are 0 experienced and no idea on how this training goes. We conquered the stress, every challenges, specially we are training in the midst of the Pandemic.


But, We have overcome the challenges through the help of our trainer, Sir Ronald Surita, and Technical Education Skills and Development Authority (TESDA)
